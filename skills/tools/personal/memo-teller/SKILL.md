# 生产队往事挖掘机 (Legacy Story Agent)

This is an oral history excavation tool specifically designed for the older generation.

### Features:
- **Guided Questioning**: Provides professional interview logic to guide elders in recalling details.
- **Story Recording**: Structurally saves oral history material.
- **Script Conversion**: Automatically generates short video/drama scripts.

### How to Use:
Simply say to Claw: "Help me interview my dad about the story of grain distribution in the production team."

# Comprehensive Introduction to Digital Nomads

This skill helps you gain a comprehensive understanding of Digital Nomads.

## What is a Digital Nomad

### Definition
Digital nomads are individuals who leverage digital technology to work remotely, free from geographical constraints. They typically:

- 🌏 Travel and work around the world
- 💻 Complete their work via the internet
- ⏰ Independently manage their work hours and locations
- 🏖️ Pursue a work-life balance

### Development History

| Year | Milestone |
|------|---------|
| 2005 | The concept of "Digital Nomad" is first proposed |
| 2010 | Remote work tools become popular |
| 2015 | Digital nomad communities emerge |
| 2020 | The pandemic drives a surge in remote work |
| 2023 | Countries launch digital nomad visas |

## Getting Started Advice

### First Step
1. Identify remote work skills
2. Establish an income stream
3. Save up funds (3-6 months)

### Second Step
1. Choose a destination (short-term)

3. Research visa requirements

### Third Step

## Precautions

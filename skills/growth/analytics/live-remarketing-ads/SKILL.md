# Live Engagement Remarketing

This strategy targets users who have already shown interest by watching your videos, increasing conversion rates through familiarity.

## Workflow (Step-by-Step)

### Step 1: Audience Creation
- Go to Meta Ads Manager -> Audiences.
- Create "Custom Audience" based on **Video**.
- Select engagement levels (e.g., watched 25%, 50%, or 95% of specific videos).
- Define retention (e.g., last 30 or 60 days).

### Step 2: Campaign Strategy
- **Objective:** Conversions (Sales or Leads).
- **Ad Set:** Target only the custom audiences created in Step 1.
- **Budget:** Usually lower than cold traffic (focus on frequency).

### Step 3: Remarketing Cadence
- **Day 1-3:** "Did you miss the class? Here is the summary."
- **Day 4-7:** Social proof and student testimonials.
- **Day 8-10:** Scarcity - "Closing soon" or "Last spots".
